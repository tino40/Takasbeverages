Our Values

The Quest For Excellence

We are determined to be the best we can be, both as a company and as individuals. And will continually strive to seek improvement, unparalleled excellence in our products and new opportunities for business growth.

A Natural Choice

Takas is committed to using, where possible, only natural ingredients in our products, with a focus on using 100% locally sourced ingredients, natural flavours and natural colourings in our products.

A Company of People

Our success lies in our company staying well-organized and efficient. The core to this is our team of highly talented and motivated staff.

Superior Quality

Quality is the foundation of Takas' activities, and cannot be ignored. Consumers should always be able to trust the superior quality and taste of our products.

It's All About Our Customers

We are in business because of our customers. We are committed to establishing and maintaining profitable partnerships with our customers by providing products they can trust, superior service and effective promotional support for our flavours.
